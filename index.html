Favorite Publication

Aparicio J (2022). Web Application for Spatial Modelling of Field Trials. R package version 2.0.8, https://github.com/AparicioJohan/MrBeanApp.

  • 01. Aim

    Develop an easy-to-use, practical and user-friendly tool for analysing field trial in agriculture.

  • 02. Method

    This app integrates the spatial modelling capabilities of SpATS, the analytical robustness and speed of ASReml, the graphical versatility of plotly and the interactive and simple construction approach offered by Shiny for the development of web applications.

  • 03. Result

    R-Shiny open source package. MrBeanApp

  • 04. Conclusion

    Mr.Bean is a novel tool that allows users to analyze single and multi-environment trials in a fast and efficient way. The app can be also used for teaching, comparing approaches, and for decision-making.
